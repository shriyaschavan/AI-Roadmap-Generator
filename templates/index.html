AI Maturity Levels Explained

Low Maturity

Organizations at this level are at the starting point of AI adoption. They typically lack structured data, dedicated AI resources, and foundational digital tools.

  • Minimal automation
  • Data is mostly unstructured or siloed
  • No formal AI strategy or governance

Medium Maturity

Organizations have begun systematic AI adoption with early wins, some automation, and better data management practices.

  • Some structured data and BI tools
  • Pilots and proof-of-concepts underway
  • Early cross-functional collaboration

High Maturity

Organizations operate with a fully integrated AI strategy, advanced analytics, automated workflows, and strong governance.

  • Enterprise-grade data infrastructure
  • AI used across multiple value streams
  • Dedicated AI/ML teams and governance

AI maturity reflects how prepared your organization is to adopt, scale, and operationalize AI technologies.
